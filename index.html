NOT ONLY A PHOTOGRAHER

BUT ALSO A LIFE RECORDER

My name is Weihua Huang. I was born and raised in China.The art of photography is part of my life. As an amateur photographer, I lay my footprints on many fabulous places with beautiful scenery, astonishing landscapes, charming people, and inspiring activities.

Currently, I am member of Global Photographic Union, Royal Photographic Society, and Photographic Society of America (China). Since April 2016, I have been actively participating 30+ international shows and contests for photography and received nominations for 160+ of my artefacts. Multiple photographs of mine were granted gold and silver medals, prizes from the nominating committee, and honor belt awards. It is my dream to see, record, read, and understand the unknown Chinese lands and cultures through my lenses, and to open an exhibition of my work under the theme of “A Different China”.

ABOUT LIANGSHAN

Liangshan Yi Autonomous Prefecture in Sichuan is located in Southwest Mountain Range in China. It is the largest traditional settlement of minority people known as “Yi” in China with more than two million Yi inhabitants. It is a beautiful place which is an ideal land for a photographer. But it is also the most poverty-stricken areas in the country. During years, I visited Liangshan more than ten times. Not only to take photographes, but also to help the poeple there, especially the children and elders.
